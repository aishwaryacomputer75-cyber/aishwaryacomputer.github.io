About Us

Established in 2021, Aishwarya Computer & Information Technology, Ambajogai is a reputed institute dedicated to empowering students with modern digital and computer skills. Our mission is to make quality computer education accessible to every learner in Ambajogai and nearby regions. We offer a variety of certified courses such as MS-CIT, Tally Prime, Typing, Programming, and Web Design.

Our Mission

To provide quality computer and IT education that builds technical competence and confidence among students, preparing them for modern career opportunities and entrepreneurship.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a leading computer education center in Marathwada, known for innovation, quality teaching, and community contribution.

The institute is managed by Ms. Rupali Dalvi (Founder & Director), with a vision to make Ambajogai a digital education hub. We focus on practical learning, real-world skills, and professional growth.
